## Justification Format

**Inherent Risk Justification Template:**
```
[Quantification]: [Calculation with $ amounts]
[Threshold]: This [exceeds/is below] the [threshold name] of $[amount].
[Likelihood]: [Process gap explanation]. As such, likelihood is [Level].
[Conclusion]: As such, the Inherent Risk Rating is [Rating].
Note: [Caveats or gaps]
```

**Pre-Mitigation Justification Template:**
```
Impact: [Assessment]. [Rationale].
Likelihood: [Assessment]. [Control effectiveness].
As such risk is assessed as [Rating].
```

## GRC Control Format

- **Control ID:** 3-digit (470, 613)
- **GRC ID:** CXXXXXX - C.[DEPT].[CODE].[###]
- **Examples:** C001702 - C.FIN.FLAR.202, C004513 - C.ICM.MTCA.115

## Risk Detection Quick Reference

| Signal | → L1 | → L2 |
|--------|------|------|
| custodian, counterparty, exposure, stress loss | Credit Risk | Counterparty Credit Risk |
| cash flow, forecast, CFF | Operational Risk | Reporting Risk |
| SAS code, EUC, logic, TCAT | Operational Risk | Technology Risk |
| MTA, Trust Agreement | Compliance Risk | Capital Markets Compliance |
| PII, data, encryption | Information Risk | Privacy / Data Management |
| vendor, third party | Third Party Risk | [varies] |
